Full stories in just six words.

Ex-girlfriend and ex-brother in matching rings.

He whispered, "Alexa, am I alone?"

Seeing her bump, frantic math ensued.

The priest looked better on Grindr.

He snored louder than her husband.

To-do: Burn clothes. Destroy phone. Forget.

His drug was romance. Hers wasn't.

She'd never forget that chalk outline.

Terrified, he realized he switched strollers.

The ambulance could drive slowly now.

She would warn his next assistant.

"Nice pentagram," they heard something say.

"Hablo español. Y sí, son reales."

He returned sunburned from sick leave.

The circling vultures provided some shade.

It was his favorite sleeping bench.

"Either quiet down or invite me."
